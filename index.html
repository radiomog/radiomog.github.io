About Unemployment Radio

Unemployment Radio is a tiny online radio “station” for people who are somewhere between jobs, plans, and identities.

It’s not trying to be professional. It’s trying to be honest, a little funny, and a place to put all the feelings that don’t fit in a LinkedIn update.
