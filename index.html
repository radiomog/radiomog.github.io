About

What is Unemployment Radio?

Unemployment Radio is an online station for in-between seasons: job hunting, career pivots, weird Tuesdays, and those days where you’re technically “free” but emotionally overloaded.

It starts with The Mog Hour, but is built to grow into a full little universe of shows — music-driven, story-driven, and very human.
